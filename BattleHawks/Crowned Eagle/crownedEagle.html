Crowned Hawk-eagle

Status: Near-threatened

by Rayed Jawad

Description

Adult crowned eagles have a dark brown head with long crest feathers that are white-tipped, a cream or reddish breast with black bars, and wings that are black on top and red below. The female is bigger than the male, as is the case with many raptors. calling out loud. The scream of crowned eagles is well recognized.
