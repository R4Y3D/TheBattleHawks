Ridgway's Hawk

Status: Critically Endangered

By: Bianca Perez



Description:

  • Bird of prey in the family Accipitridae endemic to the island of Hispanola in the Carribean
  • Classified as critically endangered because of habitat destruction and human persecution in the Dominican Republic
  • Due to conservation efforts, the population is now increasing
  • It is named in honour of the ornithologist Robert Ridgway
