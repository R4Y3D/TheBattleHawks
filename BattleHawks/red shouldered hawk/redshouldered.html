Red-Shouldered Hawk

Status : Special Concern

By: Islombek Komilov

Interesting information about the Red-Shouldered Hawk Species:

  • These Hawks are slender, crow sized birds with red shoulder patches.
  • You can tell the difference between the young and adults as the younger ones are light underneath meanwhile the adults have a darker red pattern.
  • They typically fly alone and maintain a graceful butterfly-like flight pattern.
